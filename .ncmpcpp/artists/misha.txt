There are more than one artist with this name:

Misha (group)
Misha is a collective based in New York. If they`re not traveling around they record music together, even over long distances. What they bring you as a souvenir in return is a grand mixture of electronic pop. Juan Atkins, Carl Craig are some of their heroes, but also John Lennon and Paul McCartney.

Misha (singer )
Young female singer from Slovakia. She sings in Slovak and English language, her own songs mostly. Soul and R'n'B.

Misha (drum'n'bass producer)
Drum'n'bass producer from Russia, right now living in US.
